The Perfect Chocolate Cake

Description: For those who enjoy chocolate, this tender and moist chocolate cake is heaven! The rich, dark chocolate flavor explodes in your mouth, while an enticing aroma fills the air. What is the best part? It is surprisingly simple and quick to prepare, making it ideal for those with a last-minute dessert craving.


Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • half a cup of oil
  • a bit of salt
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 3 spoons of chocolate
  • 1 measure of baking powder

Steps

  1. place the eggs in a container and add sugar
  2. beat well until well blended
  3. add a pinch of salt and flour
  4. add the chocolate
  5. mix everything well
  6. add the baking powder and mix.
  7. Bake in the oven at 180Â° for 45 minutes
