Mango Colada

Description: This mango colada is blended with mango, for an even more tropical-tasting pi√±a colada. I use frozen fruit to eliminate the use of ice.


Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 2 fluid ounces pineapple juice
  • 2 fluid ounces cream of coconut
  • 2 fluid ounces coconut milk


Steps:

  1. Place frozen pineapple, frozen mango, rum, pineapple juice, cream of coconut, and coconut milk in a blender cup and blend until smooth. Serve immediately.
    • Be sure to use cream of coconut and not coconut cream. Cream of coconut is sweeter and normally used for mixed drinks.
